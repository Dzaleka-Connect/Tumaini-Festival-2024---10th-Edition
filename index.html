About The Festival

Transforming Lives Through Arts & Culture

Founded in 2014 by Menes La Plume, Tumaini Festival has grown to become Malawi's largest multicultural event. The festival brings together refugees and locals through artistic expression.

Cultural Exchange

The festival brings together artists from over 20 countries

Community Impact

Creating opportunities for 750+ refugees annually

Our Impact

Creating Lasting Change

Cultural Preservation

Celebrating and preserving diverse cultural traditions through music, dance, and art.

  • 20+ Traditional performances
  • Cultural workshops
  • Art exhibitions

Community Building

Fostering understanding and connection between refugees and host communities.

  • 50,000+ Festival attendees
  • Cross-cultural dialogues
  • Community collaborations

Economic Empowerment

Creating sustainable economic opportunities within the refugee community.

  • 750+ Jobs created
  • 400+ Host families benefited
  • Local business growth
